Background

EDUCATION & CREDENTIALS

PASSION FOR ACTIVE ADULTS

Started my own practice after realizing the limitations in corporate healthcare. I wanted more one-on-one time with clients to help them achieve goals that exceed the recovery point set by insurance models.

EXPERTISE & SPECIALIZATIONS

Extensive experience with active adults at all levels. True passion for extremity-based injuries — knees, ankles, hips. As a runner and former athlete, I understand the grit required to stay active.
